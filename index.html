• 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozzarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter
Grilled Cheese Ingredients
  • Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  • Place bread in frying pan and fry
  • Add cheese on top of bread
  • Cover with second slice of bread
  • Turn over and fry for two minutes
